About Us

Making AI agents
accountable

We believe every AI decision in a regulated industry should have an immutable, auditable record.

Mission

Give every AI agent an audit trail that can't be erased

AI agents are making critical decisions in finance, healthcare, and pharma — approving loans, summarizing patient records, generating trial reports. But there's no record of what they actually did. Breach Intel changes that. We build the infrastructure layer that sits between your AI agent and regulatory accountability — logging every compliance violation with cryptographic proof, without changing a single line of your agent's code.

Vision

A world where AI compliance is automatic, not aspirational

We envision a future where deploying an AI agent in a regulated industry automatically comes with tamper-evident compliance logging — just like deploying a web app comes with HTTPS. No manual audits, no compliance theater, no gaps between what your agent does and what you can prove it did. Compliance should be infrastructure, not overhead.
